Making Deck

To make a deck, click the "Make New Deck" button above. This will prompt you to a
new page where you can choose which characters you would like on your deck.

Choosing characters

You can choose from over 50 hockey players! Their stats are determined through a special
algorithm based off of their weight, height, and hockey record. They vary widely in terms of weight
and height, so choose wisely. You may also choose Pokemon, who have special abilites. But be
careful, as Pokemon do not act like Hockey players. Their stats as they may act as a double-edged sword.

Deck Restrictions

You may be tempted by the vast amount of characters on the customization screen, but
watch out - you can only choose 15 characters. In order to make a valid deck, you will
need a unique deck name, as well as exactly 15 characters. Any more or less will not
allow you to complete the deck. When finished, click the finish button on the bottom
of the page.

Playing the game

To play the game, hit the "Play" button above. It will prompt you to a page named
"choose deck", where you will have to (you guessed it!) choose your deck.

Game board

The game board will feature your deck, where you can choose which character you
would like to send to battle. However, it will not show all 15 of your characters;
it will choose 3 random cards from your deck. These cards will re-shuffle after
you choose your card, throwing out the card you chose.

Battling Opponent

After you choose your card, you will fight a card that your opponent chooses.
These two cards fight, and the winner is decided through an algorithm that
takes factors in your attack and defense (and type if you are Pokemon).

Winning the Game

You and your opponent have 3 lives. The loser of each round loses one life.
Whoever reaches zero lives wins the game. Easy peasy right? If you want to play
again, you can make a new deck or use your old one.
